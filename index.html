Guidelines
🥚
Eggs — 2 days/week only
Thursday + Saturday dinner
  • Max 2 eggs per session — no more
  • Rotate styles: half fry, bhurji, omelette, boiled, shakshuka
  • Never on Tuesday (your own rule)
  • Sautéed veggies always alongside
🥩
Soya Chaap
Once every 2 weeks · Saturday lunch
  • 2 pieces masala chaap ≈ 24g protein
  • Pair with 1 roti + salad only
  • Avoid cream-heavy gravy versions
  • On the off-week use paneer rice bowl or chole
🫘
Thursday Protein Swaps
When chole isn't available
  • Rajma — same protein, easier digest
  • Soya keema sabji — highest protein (~28g)
  • Chana dal — lighter option
  • Paneer bhurji — always a fallback
🥑
Healthy Fats Daily
Don't skip these
  • 1 tsp ghee on roti — don't avoid it
  • Chia / flax seeds in oats or curd
  • 5 almonds + 2 walnuts every morning
  • Peanuts in poha / upma
💊
Creatine on Rest Days
Wednesday + Sunday
  • Still take 5g — consistency matters
  • Take with water after lunch
  • No whey on rest days — food protein is enough
  • Keep drinking 3L water even on rest days
🌙
Sunday Flex Dinner
One per week · stay controlled
  • No seconds on flex meals
  • Avoid pav with bhaji
  • Pasta — olive oil base only, no cream
  • Reset fully Monday morning
💧
Hydration
3–3.5L daily, always
  • Cinnamon warm water on wake-up
  • Creatine pulls water into muscles — stay ahead
  • Sip continuously through workout
  • Coconut water 1–2×/week is fine
⏰
Meal Timing
Gym at 5–7 PM
  • Snack at 5 PM doubles as light pre-gym fuel
  • Whey + creatine immediately post-session
  • Dinner 8–9 PM — your post-workout meal
  • Don't skip dinner even on non-gym days
